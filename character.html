Main Characters

Luigi, the main character of Luigi's Mansion, was sent a letter saying he won a mansion from a contest he never entered. When he arrived he meet Professor E. Gadd and realize that the mansion was a trap set by King Boo and he already trapped his brother Mario into a painting. So now Luigi must save Mario.

E Gadd is a ghost researcher and meet Luigi in Luigi's Mansion and help assist Luigi in finding and saving Mario from King Boo. E Gadd gives Luigi some of his inventions to assist him like the pulturgust and communication devices made to look like nintendo handheld consoles.

From a mixture of ecoplasm and green coffee by accident, E Gadd has created a weird goo creature that he later fuse with Luigi's DNA. Gooigi helps Luigi in the events of Luigi's Mansion 3. Prior to his appearence in Luigi's Mansion 3 he appeared in the 3DS remake of the original Luigi's Mansion where E Gadd sent Gooigi back in time to train his ghost hunting skills.

Antagonists

King Boo is an enemy and boss from the Mario series, and the main antagonist and final boss of the Luigi's Mansion series. He is the king of all the Boos and ghosts and the archenemy of Luigi. His most prominent ally is the Koopa King Bowser, who has aided King Boo in his various schemes. He is more than capable of devising and enacting villainous plans by himself, including his capture and imprisonment of Mario. Although not the biggest Boo, King Boo has abilities that far surpass that of the average ghost; he wields a number of impressive magical abilities, including the ability to materialize objects. King Boo's magical power is directly proportional to the number of Boos in his vicinity.

Hellen Gravely is a character making her debut appearance in Luigi's Mansion 3, where she is the owner of The Last Resort and the secondary antagonist of the game.Hellen Gravely is a huge fan of King Boo. Sometime after the events of Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon, she lures Professor E. Gadd to her hotel, then captures him and steals his ghost collection, including King Boo, who she releases.
