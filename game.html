Gameplay

In every game you explore a mansion and capture ghost. You mainly need to solve room puzzles to get to other areas and can also fins hidden treasures to collect.In Luigi's Mansion you turn you flashlight on the ghosts to stun them and then seuck them up with your polturgust. The polturgust can also suck up and spray out water and fire.

In Luigi's Mansion: Dark Moon it is similar except the new polturgust 2000 can't suck up and spray out water and fire. The polturgust 2000 however has the strobe light that is used as the new way to stun ghosts. Also instead of just moving the joystick around to suck up ghost you move back to charge the suction so when pressing the A button while sucking in the ghost the charge will increase the power of the suction for a breif moment.

The arcade is an on rail shooter.

Luigi's Mansion 3 is similar to Dark Moon but they added in Gooigi for co-op gameplay. Luigi can now also shoot a suction cup so you can drag and/or smash objects. Most of the puzzels involve switching between Luigi and Gooigi.
